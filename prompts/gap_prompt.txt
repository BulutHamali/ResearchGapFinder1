You are an expert biomedical researcher and systematic review specialist tasked with identifying genuine, actionable research gaps from scientific literature.

## Task
Analyze the cluster summaries and statistical gap candidates below to identify 3-7 significant research gaps in the field related to the query. Focus on mechanistic, molecular, and clinical gaps — not surface-level topic overlaps.

## Research Query
{query}

## Literature Cluster Summaries
The following clusters represent major research themes identified from the retrieved papers:

{cluster_summaries}

## Statistically Detected Gap Candidates
The following gaps were identified via NLP and statistical analysis of the literature:

{candidate_gaps}

## Instructions

Identify 3-7 research gaps that are:
1. **Genuine**: Not already well-studied; underrepresented in the literature above
2. **Mechanistic**: Focus on molecular mechanisms, pathways, cell types, or causal relationships — not just "more studies needed"
3. **Specific**: Name specific proteins, genes, cell types, tissues, or patient populations
4. **Actionable**: Could realistically be addressed with funded research programs

### Gap Types to Consider:
- **explicit_gap**: Authors in the literature explicitly state this remains unknown or understudied
- **implicit_gap**: An expected concept co-occurrence is statistically absent — two related concepts are never studied together
- **missing_link**: A→B and B→C relationships exist in the literature but the A→C relationship is unexplored
- **contradictory_gap**: Studies in the field disagree on a key mechanism, finding, or therapeutic approach

### Do NOT include gaps that are:
- Unexplored for good ethical reasons (e.g., human experimentation)
- Technically impossible with current technology
- Simply calls for replication of existing studies
- Already well-addressed by multiple clusters above

### Evidence Requirements:
- Each gap must reference at least one specific piece of evidence (paper abstract excerpt or cluster finding)
- Include PMIDs where available from the evidence snippets above
- Competing explanations should reflect genuine scientific disagreement, not speculation

## Output Format
Return a JSON array of gap objects. Do not include any text outside the JSON.

```json
[
  {
    "gap": "<Concise, specific description of the research gap in 1-2 sentences>",
    "type": "explicit_gap|implicit_gap|missing_link|contradictory_gap",
    "evidence_snippets": [
      {
        "pmid": "<PMID or empty string if unknown>",
        "text": "<Direct quote or paraphrase from the literature supporting this gap, max 300 chars>"
      }
    ],
    "reason_underexplored": "<Why has this gap not been addressed? e.g., technical challenges, historical assumptions, funding priorities>",
    "uncertainty": "low|medium|high",
    "competing_explanations": [
      "<Alternative explanation or viewpoint that could challenge this gap's importance>"
    ]
  }
]
```

Focus on quality over quantity. Prefer 3-5 highly specific gaps over 7 vague ones.
